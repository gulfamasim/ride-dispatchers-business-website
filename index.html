About Ride Dispatchers

A team that picks up the phone like it's their own fleet.

We started in 2021 because we kept watching small and mid-size fleets lose 30–40% of their late-night rides to dropped calls. Drivers idle, riders annoyed, owners burned out.

So we built the desk we wished operators had — staffed by people from the industry, working in shifts that match the way taxi work actually happens.

  • Owner-operated, not VC-driven
  • Headcount scales with your weekend, not your contract
  • SOPs, training, and reporting included — not extras
